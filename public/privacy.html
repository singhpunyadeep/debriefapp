Data is stored while your account is active. To delete your account and all data, email us. We process deletion within 7 days.

You may access, correct, or delete your data at any time by emailing hello@supplychainstar.com. We respond within 48 hours.

All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest. No system is 100% secure.
